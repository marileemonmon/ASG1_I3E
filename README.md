# Game Overview

Escape Room Adventure is a first-person puzzle game where the player must explore the environment, collect gems and keycards, unlock doors, avoid hazards, and reach the exit to escape successfully.

The objective is to collect enough gems and progress through the rooms while managing your health.

# Gameplay Instructions

1. Explore the environment.
2. Look at collectibles and press **E** to collect them.
3. Collect keycards to unlock doors.
4. Avoid hazards such as water and spinning blades.
5. Each hazard deals damage to the player.
6. Monitor your health using the health bar.
7. Collect all gems to reach highest score.
8. Reach the exit to complete the game.


# Collectibles

### Gems

* Increase player score.
* Required to hit the highest score.

### Keycards

* Used to unlock doors.

# Hazards

# Water

* Damages the player upon contact.

# Rotating Blade

* Damages the player upon contact.


# Win Condition

To win the game:

1. Collect all required keys.
2. Reach the exit area.
3. The victory screen will be displayed.

# Game Over Condition

The game ends when the player's health reaches 0.

A Game Over screen will be displayed.

# Puzzle Solutions / Answer Key

### Puzzle 1

Collect the required keycard to unlock Door 1

### Puzzle 2

Find the keycard in the maze and collect gems.

### Puzzle 3
Collect all the gems and required keycard to open last door and win
